# Product Positioning

Spatial AI Modeling System is positioned as a spatial product and AI modeling workflow, not as a traditional architecture design portfolio.

The project focuses on how a designer can turn site data, parcel IDs, spatial rules, and design intent into verifiable parametric modeling components. Its value is in the workflow: data preparation, rule definition, GH Python cell design, building generator patterns, Rhino/GH validation, AI prompt workflows, and GitHub packaging.

The title intentionally does not use Architecture as the main category. The system can support architectural and urban design workflows, but its product identity is broader: space product prototyping, generative modeling, design automation, and human-in-the-loop AI collaboration.

## Positioning statement

Spatial AI Modeling System is a reusable workflow for AI-assisted spatial modeling. It helps designers convert spatial data and rules into testable Rhino/Grasshopper components while keeping human validation at the center of the process.

## What makes it product-oriented

- It has reusable schemas.
- It has repeatable skills.
- It has templates and prompts.
- It has validators and checklists.
- It documents limitations and failure recovery.
- It can be packaged on GitHub as a system others can follow.
